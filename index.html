Link

Covid-19 tracking app

Link is a mobile application developed during the Covid-19 pandemic for demonstration purposes only. The app allows users to easily and securely log the people they have come into contact with. If one of the users reports testing positive for the virus, the app automatically notifies all individuals they have met in the past 14 days, alerting them of potential exposure. Likewise, users are immediately notified if anyone they’ve been in contact with later tests positive.

Sporty

Sport matchmaking app

Sporty is an innovative app designed for sports enthusiasts who want to organize matches or friendly games easily, quickly, and based on location. Through a simple and intuitive interface, users can select their preferred sport, choose a difficulty level, and specify the area of the city and the date they are available. The app then displays potential opponents or teams that match the criteria, making it easy to connect and arrange the sporting event.

Find 'Em

Web automation tool

Find 'Em is a powerful web automation tool designed to assist botters in securing limited-edition items more efficiently. It offers a wide range of specialized features, including:

  • a Queue-it module developed to improve the chances of bypassing virtual waiting rooms, equipped with an in-house system for solving text CAPTCHAs and anti–anti-bot countermeasures;
  • automatic checkout modules for platforms such as Whop, Hyper, and Discord (Nitro), featuring proprietary anti-jigging solvers;
  • a real-time offer monitor for selected Discord channels;
  • a Shopify account generator capable of creating unlimited accounts, with built-in support for automatic CAPTCHA solving;
  • a dummy data generator for jigging purposes;
  • a Vinted views booster to increase item exposure by artificially inflating view counts;
  • CSV editing tools and a profile converter compatible with various sneaker bots.
Find Em delivers a complete and optimized automation suite tailored for the demands of modern botting.

Littlestar

Pet dating app

Littlestar is a social app designed for pet owners who want to expand their furry friend's social network. Users can create a profile for their pet, upload photos and details, and discover other nearby animals registered on the platform. The app functions as a true social network, allowing owners to interact, like posts, and chat directly through the built-in messaging feature. Littlestar also makes it easy to arrange meetups in shared spaces around the city, such as parks or pet-friendly areas, encouraging fun, play, and social interaction for both the animals and their humans.

Deadlock checker

Deadlock checker for FIFO-based operating systems

This tool was developed as a school project and is based on the analysis of Holt graphs to detect potential deadlock situations in operating systems that use the FIFO (First-In, First-Out) resource allocation policy. The application takes a graph representation as input and, through a reduction algorithm, checks whether the graph is reducible. If the graph is found to be non-reducible, a possible deadlock state is flagged. This makes the tool especially useful for studying and preventing concurrency issues in operating systems. It serves as an interactive educational resource to help users understand the concept of deadlock and explore prevention techniques in shared-resource environments.
